About Our School

Founded in 2005, Landscaping Inspiration has been at the forefront of landscape education for over 15 years. Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of landscaping professionals through hands-on training and expert instruction.

Our campus features 20 acres of diverse landscape environments, including formal gardens, natural habitats, and sustainable agriculture plots. Students have access to state-of-the-art facilities including design studios, propagation greenhouses, and equipment workshops.

Our faculty consists of award-winning landscape architects, master gardeners, and horticulture experts who bring real-world experience into the classroom. We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and mentorship for every student.

Graduates of our programs have gone on to establish successful landscape businesses, work for prestigious design firms, and contribute to sustainable urban planning projects around the world.
